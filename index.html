Sudoku


Welcome to the Game!

Read the instructions carefully before you proceed...

  • Sudoku is played on a 9x9 grid
  • You can select any number from 1 to 9
  • Place the number on any row or column so that the row or column contains the numbers 1 through 9
  • BUT, no number can repeat
  • Within the 9x9 grid are 9 3x3 grids
  • They must be populated by numbers 1 through 9 but again they cannot contain the same number more than once
  • If you have filled the whole grid with numbers and still have lives, you win!
